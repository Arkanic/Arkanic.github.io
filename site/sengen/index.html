Help + Instructions

This is a game about finding the blanks in japanese sentences.

The bar at the top is the time left on the question. If it gets to the bottom you loose the question.

You gain points by answering a question correctly. Answering questions involves clicking the "pick one" box and selecting what the correct particle or word that should be.

After you answer a set amount of questions your score will appear on the leaderboard. Note that this is personal to you, and other people won't be able to see your score.
